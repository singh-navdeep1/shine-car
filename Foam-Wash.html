Foam Wash

Some car owners prefer taking their cars to specialized washing stores, while others prefer to wash and clean them at home using a mix of water and soap, rinsing the car and drying them down with a sponge and cloth. But it won’t help them to restore the car’s original gloss and shine. So, many car owners are now switching to a new type of car wash called a Foam Wash.

Foam Car Wash is the most effective way to clean the car’s exterior than soap and water. The process involves forming a blanket of foam car wash chemicals that cover the car for thoroughly cleaning. It is also referred to as a snow car wash, as the lather looks like thick snow over the exterior. The thick leather breaks the hard grime, dirt and dust from the car’s exterior and thorough washing removes them easily.

What is a Foam Car Wash?

A foam car wash is the most widely used method in car detailing shops like Sri Car Care Solutions. Foaming the car is referred to as forming a blanket of foam cleaner to cover the car’s exterior. It is also referred to as a snow car wash because of the thick lather that looks like a blanket of snow over the car's surface.

The thick lather helps loosen the hardest grime and dirt from the surface, followed by thorough washing to remove the dirt. The foam car wash is not a complete car washing but a pre-wash process. A washing cleaner is applied to the vehicle before regular washing and waxing. The cleaners are premium liquid car wash designed to restore the gloss with effective cleaning and washing without the need for scrubbing.

When Must Car Owners Use Foam Wash?

Once the dust particles are set on the car’s surface, you need to use brute force to remove them, and it causes surface damage. So, Foam Wash by Sri Car Care Solutions is important as it ensures loosening that dirt and grime for easy removal with regular washing without damaging the paintwork. Some types of particles that need foam car wash are bugs, bird droppings, and grit, whose removal can leave permanent scratches and marks on the car’s surfaces.

The professionals at Sri Car Care Solutions use a pressure washer or a hose for effective foam car washing. It helps them rinse the car thoroughly and ensure all places where the dirt and grime are trapped are properly moistened. The goal of the pressure washer is to loosen the dirt as much as possible so that the dirt can be removed with regular washing. So, when you see any dirt or grime that is hard to remove with regular washing, bring your car to Sri Car Care Solutions for professional Foam Wash. The professionals will take care of the toughest dirt without causing damage to the paintwork.
