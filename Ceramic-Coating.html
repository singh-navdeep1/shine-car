Ceramic Coating is the new development or breakthrough in paint protection that has impacted businesses globally. It is the advanced type of paintwork for your car, an approach to painting a car that makes your expectations a reality.  The concept of ceramic coating was first introduced by a professional boat manufacturing company, Cougar Boats. They invented it from their research to enhance the resin coating in 1983. Their invented product has proven effective for many years, earning international recognition amongst car owners and boat manufacturers.

How does Ceramic Coating Work?

We at Sri Car Care Solutions use only quality and standards ceramic paints for the job. The ceramic paint is applied on the exterior of your car upon existing paintwork. It is like a clear coat. Unlike standard clear coats, silicones and polymers, the ceramic coating comprises 100% ceramic or whitewall.

We have professionals with rich experience and expertise in ceramic painting, and they apply it correctly without affecting the surfaces, leaving your car cleaner and brighter than ever before. Besides, the protective layer prevents dust and dirt from sticking to the surface and deters up to 95% of UV radiation, reducing the risk of discoloration.

As a result, ceramic paint's protective elements last for years because it attracts less dirt, dust and UV radiation. Besides, our professionals' superior quality ceramic paint also offers exceptional scratch resistance. It means after ceramic coating, the need for waxing is eliminated.

Why Choose Ceramic Coating Upon Polishes and Waxing?

Conventional car care products and waxes will likely wear off as soon as you start driving and expose the surface to damaging elements, including airborne pollutants and rocks. Besides, waxes and polishes cannot protect the car paintwork against environmental elements and contaminants. These products create a sealant over the paintwork when they dry. Waxes provide protection to a certain extent, but it wears off and demands reapplication after a few months.

Frequent washing and cleaning of the exterior also lead to scratches caused by contaminants trapped under the way layers.   Unlike traditional polishes and waxes, Ceramic Coating contains zero filler or oils and it provides lasting protection for up to 2-3 years between applications. Since Sri Car Care Solutions use only high-quality, superior-grade ceramic paints, it will repel dirt, and the ceramic material won’t stick to the paintwork, making removal easier. Besides, aggressive compounds or special cleaners do not need to restore shine.
