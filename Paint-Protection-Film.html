Paint Protection Film

Every car owner wants their car to be as good and new as showroom condition. Sri Car Care Solutions Paint Protection Film is the best way to protect the car’s showroom quality finish. PPF is an indiscernible film or layer to maintain the car’s newer look for years to come without adjusting the design specs, features, or color. It protects the car’s susceptible areas, including rear fender panels, rocker panels, door edges, door handle cavities, side mirrors, hoods, and front bumper.

Made of TPU self-healing compounds and HydroSeal Top Layer, Sri Car Care Solutions PPF gives the advantage of both ceramic coating and PPF. It is backed by 200 microns thickness protection with years of guarantee. So, protect the car’s finish and showroom quality with Sri Car Care Solutions Paint Protection Film and keep it looking newer to enhance its resale value in the future. The PPF is professionally applied on the surface using the right tools that protect the exterior from chips, scratches, dust, stains and other environmental damages. The invisible layer of PPF will not change the design, specs or color elements.

Select Black PPF

Most Paint Protection Film products are designed to improvise paint’s durability and amalgamate with showroom quality finish. But, Sri Car Care Solutions Select Black PPF is different as the premium grade PPF changes the appearance of the underlying color to glossy and rich black. Besides, it offers ultimate protection and comes with multiple customization options.

Regardless of your style, you will likely have a brand new-looking car that deserves attention. The Select Black PPF offers all the protective advantages of Platinum Gloss and Matte PPF. It protects the surface against rocks, salt and other debris and has self-healing properties with scratch resistance. The HydroGard top layer ensures protection against discoloration and dirt.

Platinum Extra PPF

Platinum Extra PPF is for those car owners who want their car’s finish to withstand the toughest conditions that come with gravel roads, track racing, harsh weather and off-road driving. Platinum Extra is an undetectable, thicker film than conventional PPF, and it offers high-impact resistance and chip-fighting.

The hard-wearing defense of Platinum Extra PPF comes with many benefits and protective properties. Wrap your entire car with Platinum PPF or use it for susceptible areas like fenders, rocker panels, bumpers, hoods and doors.

Platinum Gloss PPF

Platinum Gloss PPF is the top choice for many car enthusiasts because it offers protection against damages from different threats, including sand, flying rocks, road salt, bugs, dirt and oil. Because of the high-gloss top layer that blends with the factory finish, the platinum gloss PPF is invisible and provides incredible protection to the exterior of your car.

Platinum Gloss PPF comes with self-healing properties that manage to repair minor scratches and marks caused by exposure to heat and UV rays. The HydroGard technology offers stain resistance, and it heightens the durability of the Paint Protection Film.

Gloss and Matte PPF

The car without PPF will likely incur scratches and damages affecting the factory finish. So, add a layer of Sri Car Care Solutions Gloss or Matte Paint Protection Film to sustain the original finish and factory paintwork as it protects the surface from everyday road damage. It is an invisible armor that protects the car's surface from possible road damage. It is ultra-thin and highly durable to withstand the damages caused by debris, rocks, dirt, insects and other environmental threats.
