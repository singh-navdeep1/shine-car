The treatment procedure

Every car space comes with its make and ways. The following is the treatment procedure that the best firm offers to guarantee an interior cleaning in the best way possible:

  • Dry vacuuming
  • The process starts with a dry clean of every little space of your car and ensures removing the bigger particles. It eliminates dust and becomes the base for the best car cleaning.
  • Anti-bacterial coat
  • After the vacuuming completes, protecting the car from germs is vital. The professionals go for coating an anti-bacterial layer to prevent the growth of germs in the interior.
  • The scrub
  • The stains that are hard do not go away from simple vacuuming. The experts now scrub the surface hard for the perfect seat & mat power cleaning. It ensures a new look.
  • Degreaser
  • The plastic spaces need forced cleaning for all the oil and other lubricants. It lets them be oil-free and ensures the best way to restart a new journey again in your car.
  • Restoring shine
  • The last step is ensuring your car shines the best. It comes with a spray dresser that makes your car sparkle and dazzle. It is the part that makes your investment matter!
