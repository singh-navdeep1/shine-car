Paint Protection Coating

Have you ever wondered why some cars on the road look brand new all year round, even when purchased a couple of years ago? Cleaning and waxing help, but they are equipped with a protective coating that safeguards the paintwork and helps the car shine all year round.

The stock car comes with factory paintwork, and it is good but not durable. So, to make it durable and last longer, many car owners prefer applying another protective layer of film. It is referred to as Paint Protection Coating or Teflon Coating. Sri Car Care Solutions is a leader in offering Teflon coating services to both old and brand-new cars at affordable rates.

If you want to sustain the brand-new look of your car while keeping it shining all year round, opt for the rubbing and Teflon coating services by Sri Car Care Solutions.

What is Teflon Coating?

Teflon coating, or Paint Protection Coating, is a treatment for the car’s paintwork and ensures enhancing durability while sustaining its brand-new shine for years to come.

It is a protective coating that creates a protective layer above the original car’s paintwork while adding more shine and gloss. The product used in the process is Teflon, which is the chemical form of synthetic fluoropolymer. It is non-sticky and minimizes friction on the surfaces where it is applied. The protective layer enhances the paintwork and prevents corrosion and damage under extreme conditions.

What Does the Process Include?

Teflon coating is a complicated process compared to rubbing, waxing and cleaning. It involves washing, polishing, rubbing and application of Paint Protection Coating. The professionals at Sri Car Care Solutions are experienced in the process and ensure applying multiple layers to create a protective coating over the paint job to lock its shine and gloss.

  • Car Washing – The professionals at Sri Car Care Solutions thoroughly wash the car’s exterior using shampoo and rinsing the car to ensure no residues. After the washing, the car is dried to ensure no moisture on the surface because it reduces the efficiency of polishing, which is the next step.
  • Polishing – After washing and drying, the car is sent for waxing or polishing to achieve the original gloss. Professionals perform the process multiple times to restore the original shine and gloss of the paintwork.
  • Applying Teflon Coating – After polishing and rubbing the exterior to restore the shine, a Teflon coating is applied above the paintwork. It is a liquid coating that helps in the thorough application. The professionals apply multiple layers of the coat to enhance the protection. After application, the Paint Protection Coating is allowed to settle, followed by a final rubbing session without waxing.

Why Opt for Paint Protection Coating by Sri Car Care Solutions?

  • Dusting and cleaning the car regularly will help sustain its gloss and shine, but it causes micro scratches on the paintwork. So, applying the Paint Protection Coating on the paintwork surface is easy to remove the dust and dirt without causing any micro scratches.
  • Water stains cause damage to the paintwork of your car. But, if your car is equipped with Teflon coating, you don’t have to worry about any water damage because it prevents the water from causing stains as it rolls away easily from the surface.
  • All types of minor and micro scratches can be removed easily with Teflon coating. However, the deep scratches penetrated the paintwork and can’t be removed with Teflon coating.
  • The Teflon coating protects your car's paint surface from environmental elements' effects, sustaining the pristine look and shine for years to come.

What is the Use of Paint Protection Coating?

As already said, Teflon Coating or Paint Protection Coating can offer multiple benefits without making a dent in your pocket. At Sri Car Care Solutions, car owners can get a professional Teflon coating at a very nominal price, which will protect the car’s paintwork and enhance the gloss and shine for years to come. It makes minor and micro scratches invisible and prevents corrosion. The Teflon coating is also prescribed for underbody coating, and it helps the car’s undersides from corrosion and sustains the paintwork for years.

Maintenance Tips for Paint Protection Coating

If you have done the Paint Protection Coating at Sri Car Care Solutions, these maintenance tips will help you make the coating last for years.

  • Ensure to clean and wash your car paint surface regularly
  • You must not use any kind of abrasive over the coating as it will cause damage to the coat
  • Microfiber cloth must be used for cleaning the surface
  • Use only high-quality and standard polish
  • Wipe off the surface after the car is removed from the rain
